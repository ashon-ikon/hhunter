How to Use

  • Blue dashed border = your focus ZIPs (77021, 77088) — hover for detailed stats.
  • Grey polygons = no data (ZIP filtered out or too few properties).
  • Gentrification score combines YOY %, permit rate, and investor activity into one number.
  • Flip score is highest where properties trade below replacement cost, are unrenovated, and have long holding periods.
  • All maps use SFR / condo (A1/A2) properties only, excluding pending appraisals.
  • Regenerate maps after re-ingesting new HCAD data drops.
